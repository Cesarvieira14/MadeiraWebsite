Activities

Cable Car

Soar above Madeira's stunning landscape on a thrilling cable car ride. Enjoy breathtaking views of the coastline and lush greenery as you ascend to new heights.

Farmers Market

Experience the vibrant local culture at Madeira's Farmers Market. Discover fresh produce, traditional crafts, and the lively atmosphere of this bustling marketplace.

Veu da Noiva

Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of Veu da Noiva, Madeira's iconic waterfall. Marvel at the cascading waters plunging into the verdant valley below, creating a mesmerizing natural spectacle.

Levada Walks

Embark on an unforgettable adventure through Madeira's enchanting levadas. Explore lush forests, tranquil streams, and breathtaking vistas as you trek along these historic irrigation channels.

Botanical Gardens

Immerse yourself in the diverse flora of Madeira at its stunning botanical gardens. Discover exotic plants, vibrant flowers, and peaceful pathways as you wander through these botanical wonders.

Funchal Old Town Tour

Step back in time and explore the charming streets of Funchal's Old Town. Admire historic architecture, quaint cobblestone alleys, and bustling markets in this picturesque neighborhood.

Toboggan Ride

Experience a thrill like no other with a toboggan ride down the streets of Monte. Hold on tight as expert drivers navigate traditional wicker sleds through narrow streets, offering a unique and exhilarating journey.

Pico Ruivo Hike

Challenge yourself with a hike to the summit of Pico Ruivo, Madeira's highest peak. Trek through rugged terrain and dramatic landscapes to reach breathtaking panoramic views from the island's pinnacle.

Camara de Lobos

Discover the picturesque charm of Camara de Lobos, a traditional fishing village nestled along Madeira's coast. Stroll along the harbor, mingle with locals, and savor fresh seafood at waterfront restaurants.
